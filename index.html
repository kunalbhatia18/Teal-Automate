FAQs

No. Paste one line of code. We handle routing, calendars, CRM integration, analytics and weekly tests.

Forms can stay. Most teams add booking on high intent pages and keep forms for everything else. Our AI follows up via chat and email.

Which calendars do you support?

Connect Google or Microsoft calendars once; we route across your team with buffers and reminders. We also sync with any CRM to keep data in one place.

Do you store PII?

Only what is needed for scheduling and qualifying. Export on request. Compliant with GDPR and CCPA; SOC 2 in progress.

How do you optimise our pipeline?

We run weekly experiments on scripts, prompts and routing to lift conversion rates and share analytics so you can see the impact.
